Empowerment 

The Challenge

Widows and vulnerable individuals often face economic exclusion, limited job opportunities, and financial instability. 

Our Response
Our Empowerment Wing provides practical training and tools that allow individuals to build sustainable livelihoods. 

Training programs include:
Tailoring and fashion design
Sustainable agriculture
Digital literacy and small business skills
Participants also receive starter tools to begin their own micro-enterprises.

Impact Goal
Help participants transition from financial dependency to independent income generation.

VIBRANT HOPE CARE FOUNDATION is an independent Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO). While we share a spiritual heritage with Sword of the Spirit Ministry Ltd, the CIO operates as a distinct legal entity with independent financial controls, a separate bank account, and an autonomous Board of Trustees.

Corporate Structure

Vibrant Hope Care Foundation operates as an independent Charitable Incorporated Organisation (CIO) established to provide humanitarian assistance, community empowerment programs, and social support services to vulnerable communities in the United Kingdom and Nigeria.

The Foundation is governed by a Board of Trustees responsible for the strategic direction, financial oversight, regulatory compliance, and overall accountability of the organisation. The Board ensures that all activities of Vibrant Hope Care Foundation are carried out in the best interests of the public and in accordance with the legal and regulatory requirements applicable to charities in England and Wales.

Independence and Relationship Statement

Vibrant Hope Care Foundation shares a spiritual heritage with Sword of the Spirit Ministry Ltd. However, the Foundation operates as a separate and independent legal entity with its own governance framework and financial controls.

To ensure full transparency and independence, the following safeguards are in place:

Independent Governance
Vibrant Hope Care Foundation is managed by its own Board of Trustees who are solely responsible for the charity’s decisions, operations, and strategic direction.

Financial Separation
The Foundation maintains its own dedicated bank account and financial management system. Charitable funds are held and administered independently and are not co-mingled with the accounts of Sword of the Spirit Ministry Ltd.

Operational Independence
All charitable programs, fundraising activities, and operational decisions are conducted independently under the authority of the Board of Trustees of Vibrant Hope Care Foundation.

Regulatory Compliance
The Foundation operates in accordance with the governance standards and reporting requirements applicable to charitable organisations in England and Wales, ensuring transparency, accountability, and responsible stewardship of donated funds.

These safeguards ensure that all resources received by Vibrant Hope Care Foundation are used exclusively to support its charitable objectives and to deliver meaningful assistance to the communities it serves.

The Board of Trustees regularly reviews governance procedures and operational practices to maintain high standards of accountability and ensure that the Foundation continues to operate with integrity, transparency, and public benefit.

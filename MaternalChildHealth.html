Empowering Nursing Mothers and Infants in Nigeria

Conditional Cash Support
We provide financial assistance to nursing mothers who attend antenatal care and deliver in health facilities. This reduces unsafe home births and encourages supervised, safe deliveries.

Maternal & Child Health Outreach Weeks
Through community health campaigns, we deliver vital services such as immunizations, vitamins, mosquito nets, and health education. These outreach programs bring healthcare directly to families, especially in underserved areas.

Data & Program Tracking
We maintain records of maternal health interventions to identify gaps and successes. This helps us improve services and ensure accountability in our mission to protect mothers and infants.

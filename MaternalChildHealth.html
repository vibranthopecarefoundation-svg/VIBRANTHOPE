Our Care Wings

Maternal & Child Health Wing

Supporting nursing mothers and newborns by providing maternal care kits and nutritional support.


2026 Impact Goal:
Provide 1,000 maternal care kits to new mothers in underserved communities.

Empowerment Wing

Providing skills acquisition and vocational training for widows and vulnerable individuals to promote long-term financial independence.

Programs include:

Tailoring
Sustainable agriculture
Digital literacy training

Golden Age Wing

Supporting elderly individuals who lack consistent family or community support through food assistance, health monitoring, and social care.
